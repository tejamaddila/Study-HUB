🎯 Resume Tips

Make Your Resume Stand Out

01

Keep it to 1 Page

Freshers should always stick to a single page. Recruiters spend 7 seconds on average — make every line count.

02

Use Strong Action Verbs

Start every bullet with words like "Developed", "Designed", "Implemented", "Optimized".

03

Quantify Everything

"Reduced load time by 40%" beats "Improved performance". Numbers grab attention and prove impact.

04

Tailor for Each Job

Copy keywords from the job description and mirror them in your resume. ATS systems filter by keyword match.
